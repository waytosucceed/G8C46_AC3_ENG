Idioms

Spill the beans: To reveal a secret or disclose confidential information, often unintentionally.
Example: We were planning a surprise party for Sarah, but John accidentally spilled the beans during lunch.

Steal someone's thunder: To take credit for someone else's idea or to divert attention from someone else's accomplishments
Example: Jessica had been working hard on her project, but when her boss praised Mark's similar idea, he really stole her thunder.

Sink or swim: To succeed or fail based on one's own efforts; to be left to manage on one's own without assistance.
Example: After the training period, the new employees were thrown into the deep end and told it was sink or swim from there.
